About

Acrylic - Mixed Media - Pen & Ink

Bearing the creative lineage of his father, Jeremy's
deep appreciation for art began at an early age.
He has many works in many different media.
He enjoys creating original works themed upon
anything from Classical Fairy Tales,
to the darkest of the dark.
Highlighting the unusual in the every day.
Bringing light to what is dark,
And dark to what is light.
